# Code of Conduct

## Commitment

We are committed to a professional, respectful, and collaborative environment for everyone involved in this project.

## Expected behavior

- Objective, respectful communication without personal attacks.
- Technical feedback focused on problems and solutions.
- Active collaboration to maintain quality, security, and clarity.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation.
- Exposure of sensitive information without authorization.
- Hostile conduct that harms teamwork.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in contribution restrictions or access removal, based on maintainer review.

## Reporting

To report incidents, contact: `security@airport-bus.local`.
